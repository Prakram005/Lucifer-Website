About Lucifer

Bored with his life as the Lord of Hell, Lucifer Morningstar abandons his throne for the glitzy playground of Los Angeles, where he opens an upscale nightclub called Lux. His retirement takes an unexpected turn when he becomes a civilian consultant for the LAPD, using his supernatural ability to extract hidden desires to help Detective Chloe Decker solve murders. Over six seasons, the series blends witty procedural drama with an epic celestial saga as Lucifer grapples with his dysfunctional heavenly family and his own redemption. Originally airing on FOX and famously saved by fans for a final run on Netflix, the show remains a global phenomenon exploring free will and the human heart.
